About Me

I am a Software Engineer with a background in Website/E-Commerce Management. Together they have allowed me to expand my skills to not only software development but also include project management, budgeting, marketing and content management. What I found during my career was a natural curiosity towards the full cycle of the web development process. This curiosity led me to a decision to refocus my career. After taking some time off to travel, it was time to pursue that passion.

This led me to General Assembly's Software Engineering Bootcamp. Here not only did I learn some new technologies but I was able to use them by developing some full-stack applications.

By putting these new skills together with my existing skills I look forward to joining a team and build some exciting web applications.
